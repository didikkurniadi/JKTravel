About Us

Explore all tour of the Jakarta city with us.

JKTravel is a website that provides information about tours in the city of Jakarta. There are not a few tours in the capital city of Indonesia, so many visitors both from within and outside the city are confused "which tour to go to when in Jakarta?". Therefore, JKTravel was created, so that people can find out what tours are in Jakarta, and information about these tours.

  • Location

    The city of Jakarta consists of various locations, including : Central Jakarta, West Jakarta, East Jakarta, South Jakarta and East Jakarta.

  • Type

    There are various types of tourism in the city of Jakarta, including : Nature Tourism, Family Tourism, and Historical Tourism.

  • Price

    There are various prices to enter tourist areas, ranging from free to paid.
